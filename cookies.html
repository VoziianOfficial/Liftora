1. Overview

Liftora may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to keep the website functional, remember basic preferences, understand website performance, and support secure platform operation.

Liftora is an independent garage door matching platform. Liftora does not perform garage door repair, installation, replacement, inspection, manufacturing, or contracting services.

2. What Cookies and Local Storage Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. Local storage is a browser-based storage feature that allows a website to remember simple information on your device.

These technologies can help a website remember preferences, keep basic features working, support security, and improve user experience.

3. How Liftora Uses Cookies and Similar Technologies

Liftora may use cookies or local storage for the following purposes:

  • Remembering whether you accepted the cookie notice.
  • Supporting basic website functionality.
  • Helping pages, menus, forms, and interactive elements work correctly.
  • Improving website performance and user experience.
  • Protecting the website from misuse, spam, or technical abuse.
  • Understanding general website usage where analytics tools are used.

4. Essential Cookies and Local Storage

Essential storage is used to keep the site working properly. For example, Liftora may remember that you accepted the cookie notice so the banner does not appear every time you visit.

Essential cookies or local storage do not require the same kind of optional consent as marketing cookies because they support basic site functionality.

5. Analytics and Performance

Liftora may use analytics or performance tools to understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, which features are used, and whether technical errors occur.

Analytics information is generally used to improve website structure, performance, accessibility, and usability. Where analytics tools use cookies or similar technologies, they may collect technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, and interaction data.

6. Third-Party Tools

Some website features may rely on third-party tools, scripts, fonts, icons, hosting services, security systems, analytics tools, email delivery tools, or embedded resources.

Third-party tools may use their own cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. Liftora does not control all third-party storage practices.

7. Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete cookies, clear local storage, or receive alerts when cookies are used.

If you block or delete cookies and local storage, some website features may not work as intended. For example, the cookie notice may reappear after stored preferences are deleted.

To reset the Liftora cookie notice manually, clear your browser storage for this website or remove the stored cookie notice preference.

8. Request Form and Cookie Use

Submitting a garage door request through Liftora does not depend on optional marketing cookies. The request form may use basic technical functionality to validate fields, prevent spam, and submit your request.

Information submitted through the request form is handled according to the Privacy Policy.

9. Provider Websites and Cookies

If you interact with independent providers or third-party websites, those providers or third parties may use their own cookies, local storage, tracking tools, or privacy practices.

Liftora is not responsible for provider websites, provider cookie practices, provider privacy policies, or provider communication systems.

10. Updates to This Cookie Policy

Liftora may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Continued use of the website after updates means you acknowledge the revised Cookie Policy.
