Service questions

Clear answers before you continue with a provider.

These questions help homeowners understand how Liftora works as an independent matching platform and what details should be reviewed directly with providers.

Start request form
01 Do I need to know exactly what service I need?

No. You can start with the closest category and describe what you noticed. Providers can supply evaluation details, scope, pricing, timing, and terms directly.

02 What should I include in the request?

Include the door behavior, visible damage, opener symptoms, timing needs, ZIP or city, and any product or style preferences that may help structure the request.

03 Does Liftora perform garage door services directly?

No. Liftora is an independent matching platform. Garage door services, pricing, credentials, warranties, timelines, and service terms are supplied by providers and should be reviewed directly with them.

04 What should I compare before choosing a provider?

Review quote scope, included parts or products, timing, warranty terms, credentials, insurance where relevant, permits or local requirements, and provider-supplied conditions.

05 Can I use this section for any garage door service category?

Yes. The same request logic works for repair, installation, replacement, opener services, spring and cable concerns, and track, roller, or panel repair requests.
