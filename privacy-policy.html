Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Liftora collects, uses, and handles information submitted through this independent garage door matching platform.

Last updated: July 4, 2026 Independent Garage Door Matching Platform

Policy sections

Overview Information We Collect How We Use Information How Information May Be Shared Cookies and Local Storage Data Retention Security Your Choices Contact

1. Overview

Liftora is an independent garage door matching platform. Liftora helps homeowners organize garage door request details before comparing provider-supplied options from independent local providers.

Liftora does not perform garage door repair, installation, replacement, inspection, manufacturing, or contracting services. Provider availability, pricing, credentials, licensing, insurance, warranties, timelines, and service terms are supplied by participating providers and should be reviewed directly with them.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you submit directly through the website, including:

  • Name.
  • Email address.
  • Phone number.
  • ZIP code, city, or location details.
  • Selected garage door service category.
  • Project details, request notes, timing preferences, or other information you choose to submit.

We may also collect basic technical information, such as browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, form submission metadata, and information needed to keep the website functional and secure.

3. How We Use Information

Information submitted through Liftora may be used to:

  • Receive and review garage door request submissions.
  • Organize the request around a relevant garage door category.
  • Support request matching and provider comparison where applicable.
  • Contact you about your submitted request.
  • Respond to platform questions or support messages.
  • Improve website functionality, security, and user experience.
  • Maintain records needed for platform administration, legal compliance, and abuse prevention.

4. How Information May Be Shared

If you submit a garage door request, information may be shared with participating providers or relevant service partners where needed to facilitate request matching or provider follow-up.

Providers are responsible for their own pricing, availability, credentials, licensing, insurance, warranties, timelines, and service terms. You should review provider-supplied details directly before choosing how to continue.

We may also share information when required by law, to protect the website, to prevent fraud or misuse, to enforce applicable terms, or in connection with business operations such as hosting, email delivery, analytics, and technical service providers.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Liftora may use essential cookies or local storage to support basic website functionality, such as remembering whether you accepted the cookie notice.

The site may also use standard technical tools that help understand website performance, detect errors, prevent misuse, or improve the user experience. Browser settings may allow you to block or delete cookies, but some website features may not function as intended.

6. Data Retention

We keep submitted information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including request handling, platform administration, legal compliance, security, and dispute prevention.

Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the request, legal requirements, operational needs, and whether continued storage is needed to protect the platform or users.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, email system, hosting environment, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You should avoid submitting sensitive personal information that is not necessary for a garage door request.

8. Your Choices

You may contact Liftora to ask about your submitted information, request corrections, or ask that certain information be removed, subject to applicable legal, operational, and security limitations.

You may also choose not to submit the request form. If you do not provide required contact or request details, Liftora may not be able to process the request or respond to your message.

9. Third-Party Provider Interactions

Any communication, quote, service discussion, scheduling, warranty, payment, or project arrangement with a provider is between you and that provider. Providers may have their own privacy practices and terms.

Liftora does not control provider websites, provider communications, provider data practices, or provider service terms. Review provider information directly before continuing.

10. Children’s Privacy

Liftora is intended for use by adults seeking garage door request-matching information. The website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the website after updates means you acknowledge the revised policy.
