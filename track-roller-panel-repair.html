What You Can Prepare Before Submitting a Track, Roller, or Panel Request

  • Describe dents, bent tracks, damaged panels, gaps, or scraping areas.

Provider questions

Questions to Ask Track, Roller, and Panel Providers Before Continuing

Review the repair scope, parts or panels involved, timing, warranty terms, credentials, and provider-supplied details directly.

  • What track, roller, or panel issue is being evaluated?
  • What parts, panels, or hardware may be involved?
  • Is repair or replacement being discussed?
  • What timing is available?
  • What warranty or service terms apply?
  • Should credentials, insurance, or local requirements be reviewed?
