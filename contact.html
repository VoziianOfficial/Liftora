Request detail helper

What to Include in Your Request

You do not need to diagnose the garage door issue. Include what you noticed, what changed, and what you want to compare.

  • When the issue started Was it sudden, gradual, after weather, or after normal use?
  • Door movement behavior Describe stopping, shaking, scraping, uneven movement, or heavy movement.
  • Opener symptoms Remote, keypad, motor, sensor, chain, belt, or smart opener behavior.

When the issue started

Include whether the issue began suddenly, became worse over time, followed a loud noise, appeared after weather, or happened during normal door movement.

Door movement behavior

Describe whether the door opens fully, stops halfway, closes unevenly, shakes, scrapes, feels heavy, or does not respond as expected.

Opener symptoms

Note whether the remote, keypad, motor, safety sensor, chain, belt, or smart opener behaves differently than expected.

Visible damage

Include dents, bent tracks, damaged panels, loose-looking cables, gaps, scraping areas, or visible alignment issues without forcing or adjusting the door.

Replacement preferences

For replacement requests, mention preferred material, color, insulation, style, opener compatibility, and whether the current door has repeated issues.
